**Access:** Signed in and Optional/Beta

This page explains how to manage your Credits, plans, profile, and saved content.

## Account page overview

**Access:** Signed in

The **Account** page can include:

- Your user information
- **Subscribe & Save** plans, if enabled
- **Buy Credits**
- **Invite friends and earn credits**
- **Purchase History**
- **Sign Out**

You can also open **Account Settings** from your own profile header.

## Buy Credits

**Access:** Signed in

Mint currently shows these credit packs:

- `$5` for `5,000` Credits
- `$20` for `21,000` Credits
- `$50` for `55,000` Credits

What you may also see:

- A first-purchase discount banner
- Card checkout
- `USDC` checkout as an optional feature if enabled on your account

Use the buying flow inside Mint as the source of truth for payment details.

## Optional/beta: Subscribe & Save

**Access:** Optional/Beta

If subscription plans are enabled for your account, Mint may show:

- **Explorer**: `$9.99/mo`, `5` Worlds, `25` previews, `5` 3D Models per month
- **Creator**: `$24.99/mo`, `15` Worlds, `100` previews, `15` 3D Models per month
- **Pro**: `$49.99/mo`, `30` Worlds, `200` previews, `30` 3D Models per month

You may also see:

- **Manage Plan**
- Remaining monthly usage
- Past-due warnings if a payment fails

If your plan is past due, generation can be paused until billing is fixed.

## Invite friends

**Access:** Signed in

Open the invite section on **Account** to send an email invite.

Current visible referral reward:

- You earn `750` Credits
- Your friend earns `750` Credits when they sign up

Basic flow:

1. Open **Account**.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. Enter your friend's email address.
4. Click **Send**.

## Your profile

**Access:** Signed in

Your **Profile** is where Mint collects everything you have made or saved.

Main tabs can include:

- **Worlds**
- **3D Models**
- **Favorites**
- **Previews**
- **Hidden**

Other people can open your public profile too, but they only see what is visible to them.

## Worlds tab

**Access:** Signed in

Your **Worlds** tab shows:

- Finished Worlds
- In-progress Worlds
- Preview-ready Worlds

Open a finished World to explore or share it. If something is still processing, you can usually jump back into **Create** to continue.

## 3D Models tab

**Access:** Signed in

Your **3D Models** tab shows:

- Finished 3D Models
- In-progress model generations

Open a finished model to:

- View it full screen
- Turn on auto-rotate
- **Copy link**
- **Download GLB**

## Favorites tab

**Access:** Signed in

Your **Favorites** tab shows Worlds you saved from the viewer.

Use this when you want a private shortlist of places to revisit.

## Previews tab

**Access:** Signed in

The **Previews** tab is for cancelled or failed items that still have a usable Preview.

You can:

- Click **Continue** to return to **Create**
- Click **Delete** to remove the item permanently

This is the easiest recovery path when a generation did not finish the first time.

## Hidden tab

**Access:** Signed in

The **Hidden** tab shows Worlds you removed from your public profile.

Use it when:

- You want to keep a World accessible to yourself
- You do not want it shown in your public profile grid

To unhide a World, open it and use **World Options** again.

## Manage 3D Model privacy

**Access:** Signed in

Finished 3D Models are public by default unless you change them.

To change visibility:

1. Open your **Profile**.
2. Go to **3D Models**.
3. Click the gear icon on a model card.
4. Open **Model Info**.
5. Click **Make Private** or **Make Public**.

Use **Private** when you do not want others to see the model in your public profile or model library.

## Example account routine

**Access:** Signed in

1. Check your Credits.
2. Review anything in **Previews**.
3. Open **Favorites** to revisit saved Worlds.
4. Make older models **Private** if they were only for testing.
5. Send an invite if you want more Credits.
