**Access:** Public

Use this page when you need direct help with Mint.

## Contact Mint

Email [hello@mint.gg](mailto:hello@mint.gg) for support.

When you write, include any details that can help Mint understand the issue:

- What you were trying to do.
- The email address you use for Mint, if you are signed in.
- Links to the relevant World, 3D Model, Preview, Profile, or Live Room.
- Any error message you saw.
- Your browser, device, or app version if the issue happened in Mint Capture.

## Common support topics

You can contact Mint about:

- Account access and sign-in issues.
- Credits, billing, plans, or purchase history.
- Failed or cancelled World and 3D Model generations.
- Upload, Preview, sharing, embed, or download issues.
- Profile, Favorites, privacy, or hidden content questions.
- Live Room access or voice issues.
- Copyright, privacy, or Terms of Service questions.
